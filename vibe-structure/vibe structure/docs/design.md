\# Layout Pattern  

Single-page app with no navigation chrome. Content centers on a vertical flow: app title at top, personalized "Welcome, \[User Name]" message, followed by a scrollable card list of towns from the GeorgTown table. This clean, centered pattern suits focused tasks and small datasets, minimizing distractions.
